Hi, my full name is Nassouh Kabbani. Below is a short summary of my life that tells you who I am and what motivates me. Enjoy!

  • I am half Lebanese half English. So yes, half of me loves good food, and the other half doesn't.
  • I grew up in Manchester, where I spent the first 18 years of my life. I played football for Manchester United from age 6 to 12 and then I was dropped from the team and switched to Manchester City and stayed there for 4 years.
  • I am lucky to have 2 great parents and one (great) sister. We've had many arguments, most originating from Monopoly. I've now been banned from playing Monopoly with my family.
  • I went to a local private school: The Manchester Grammar School. I learned a lot there - more on the social side. It made me humbler. I usually had to miss 1-2 days of school a week for football, which I timed perfectly with art class.
